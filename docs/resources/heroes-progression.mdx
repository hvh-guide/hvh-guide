# Heroes Overview

Each hero gains a variety of buffs at certain level milestones. There are Global Buffs (Levels 30, 60, 100) which affect
ALL heroes at ALL times and a Faction Buff (Level 140) for heroes of that specific faction.
Each hero also has a passive that increases based on their star rank
