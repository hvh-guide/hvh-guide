# Dungeon Event Guide  
  
Dungeon is a weekly event that revolves around killing a set number of enemies within 10 minutes. You can use any hero for it, though some heroes do a lot better than others.
Fire Wizard and Assassin are considered the best for this event. Assassin can AFK since you don't need to target enemies.
Other AFK heroes such as Barbarian and Clockwork Mage also do really well.
Pushback based heroes fair poorly here: Knight & Paladin.

Full Rogue + Ascended Wizard's Boots (for XP attraction) / Ranger Boots, with the following jewels: assassin, vulnerability, ultimate charge, ultimate duration, cooldown and area of effect.
Recommended weapons include: Bombs + Area tome, Fire Orbs + Duplicator, Lightning + Cooldown, Crossbow, Damage Tome
You can choose to take XP tome to get your weapons online quicker, or go for Crit if you need more damage. You may also choose to drop fire orbs and take another projectile weapon like Poison Darts.
Do not take Hammer and evolve it as pushback will reduce your score.

Your score will determine your rewards and promotion to the next rank for better rewards. If you find it difficult to reach the gold tier of rewards, it would be advisable to demote yourself in the following week so you can achieve gold rank in the lower league to receive the full list of rewards instead of a partial set of rewards. Dungeon difficulty is based on the rank of the league you are currently in.
